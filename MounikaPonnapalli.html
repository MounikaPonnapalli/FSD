State university in Andhra Pradesh

The primary objective of establishing RGUKT was to provide high quality educational opportunities for the aimed rural youth of Andhra Pradesh. The initial goal was that at least the top 1 % of the rural graduates would be given the opportunity to study at RGUKT.

This website contain the information of college

The Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge & Technology campus at RK Valley, Andhra Pradesh was established in 2008. RGUKT is an autonomous university, set by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh & is recognized by the UGC. It offers an integrated BTech program in 7 disciplines, MTech & PhD courses which are full time, residential programs. Admissions to these courses are based on RGUKT CET & other Entrance examination scores. RGUKT has 3 other campuses at Nuzvid, Srikakulam & Ongole..
